About Training & Placement

Training and Placement Cell is established under the supervision of a Faculty in charge (FIC) to act as a catalyst in providing students with various facilities that support career decision making, exploration and building and provide opportunities to develop confidence and skills that are necessary to choose proper and right career. Apart from providing top class education with the novel method of teaching and learning through Information and Communication Technology (ICT), T & P Cell would help the students in choosing proper and right career.

The Training and Placement Office encompass departmental faculty representative and Student Committees comprises of diligent student representatives working under the guidance of highly experienced faculty members. The team works toward a common goal of achieving desired placement offers for all the students. Also, it is aimed at building long term corporate relations for the University. The task of the placement committee are facilitating the placement process and serves as the interface between the students and the corporate world. The core committee members work under the different functional teams to ensure smooth functioning of the placement process.
