Admission Process

  1. Admissions to the first year of Integrated B Tech Program will be based on merit in the Grade Point Average (GPA) and Grade obtained in each subject, and by following the statutory reservations of the State. A deprivation score of 0.4 prescribed by the Government for every year under Statute 13 (3) shall be added to the 10th class GPA of those applicants who studied in nonresidential Government Schools including the Zilla Parishad and Municipal Schools, with an objective of providing weight-age to the socio economically challenged students in the admission process.

  2. At RGUKT, Basar, admissions to 85% of the total available seats shall be reserved for the Local Candidates (Osmania University area, Telangana State) and the remaining 15% of the seats shall be unreserved (these seats will be filled with both States Telangana and Andhra Pradesh students based on merit) as specified in the Presidential Order 371 Article D in consonance to section 95 of the A.P. Reorganization Act, 2014.

  3. Admission into M.Tech (Computational Engg.) is through GATE examination and through PGECET conducted by State Govt. of Telangana.
