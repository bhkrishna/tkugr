Company Registration For Visit

  1. All interested companies are requested to visit the RGUKT website (http://www.rgukt.ac.in/placement/) and should register company details from COMPANY LOGIN option. Else they can respond by email (tnp@rgukt.ac.in) to correspondence with the T&P Cell.
  2. After successful registration details, Training & Placement Cell would send login and password for accessing Job Notification Form (JNF) and other details.
  3. Authorized persons of the company should fill up the details in Job Notification Form (JNF) and send it to T&P Cell of RGUKT (tnpbpo@rgukt.in/ fictnp@rgukt.ac.in) for further process.
  4. Companies can also use JNF available on website/ sent along with invitation and send filled JNF by post/email/fax/ to the T&P Cell, RGUKT-Basar.
  5. JNF provides the information on job description and other details. It is therefore, highly desirable that the form is completed in all respects. Also company can send relevant information for sharing with the students.
  6. A mutually convenient date for company visit will be finalized through consultation with the T&P Cell.
  7. The Job Announcement (JA) will be notified to the students and company posters (if sent) will be displayed at on notice boards.
  8. Eligible and interested students should submit their applications either T & P Cell format or company format (if specified).
  9. Based on the list of students submitted to the company, short listed candidates should be informed by email ( tnpbpo@rgukt.in).
  10. Recruitment for selection process (PPT, Schedule of visit, system requirements for online test, meeting rooms and other) should be informed.
  11. Guesthouse facilities available inside the campus/ nearest hotel and local transport from railway station from the campus shall be provided to company officials on prior information.
