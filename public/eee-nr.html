PROJECTS
  • Master’s Thesis: Analysis, operation and control of self-excited induction generators for stand-alone and grid connected applications.
    • Guides:
      1. Dr. N. Kumaresan, NIT, Tiruchirappalli, India. (Internal)
      2. Dr. G. Narayanan, IISc, Bangalore, India. (External)
  • Description: The analysis of self-excited induction generators was made for wind driven applications using genetic algorithm optimization method. Three different closed loop configurations, all of them employing SEIGs, have been developed, one for stand-alone and the other two for grid connected application of wind driven systems. The successful working of the proposed systems was tested through analysis, simulation and hardware implementation.
