  1. Applications from students (Current/passed out) for all the certificates mentioned in Table.1 will be accepted only up to 01:00 PM on any working day. Any student approaching after this time will be asked to submit the application on the next working day. Once the duly filled and signed application is accepted, the time required by the examination section for issuing the certificate/Grade card will be a minimum of 7 working days.
  2. All the certificates mentioned in Table.1 will also be provided on tatkal basis. However, the fee payable will be Rs.2000/- in addition to the fee payable on regular mode. Once the duly filled and signed application is accepted, the examination section will issue the certificate on tatkal mode in 2 working days.
  3. The Candidates who desire to take the Duplicate Degree Certificate/CMM/Grade card need to submit a signed application form along with the following attachments:
    1. Original copy of Police Report stating the original degree certificate/CMM is not traced.
    2. Photocopy of ID Proof.
    3. Photocopy of Provisional Certificate / Degree Certificate.
    4. Affidavit attested by First Class Magistrate on Rs. 20/- Non-Judicial bond-paper 
  4. Duplicate Provisional certificate cannot be issued under any circumstances.
