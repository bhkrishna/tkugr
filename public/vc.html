Prof. V. Venkata Ramana M.B.A., Ph.D.

Vice Chancellor I/c for RGUKT Basar

Vice-Chairman Telangana State Council of Higher Education
Email
: vc@rgukt.ac.in


Prof. V. Venkata Ramana born in Hyderabad, holds an M.B.A. and Doctoral degree in Management and received advanced training at IIIEE, Sweden and IC2 Institute, University of Texas. Prior to his present assignment he was the most senior Professor at School of Management Studies, University of Hyderabad, where he also served as Dean for more than seven years and has over 30 years experience spanning across different sectors’ academics, industry and Board level positions at State Bank of Hyderabad, SBH (4 years) and India Infrastructure Finance Company (IIFCL- Ministry of Finance, Govt. of India - 3 years).

Prof. Venkata Ramana began his career as a Research Assistant at Institute of Public Enterprise and later worked as Senior Executive at Mahindra Nissan Alwyn Ltd and as a faculty member at Osmania University before joining the University of Hyderabad in 1999.

He has extensively worked in the area of innovation, incubation and Entrepreneurship and was the founder of the TBI (Technology Business Incubator @ University of Hyderabad) and was Executive Council Member and held many administrative positions in the University of Hyderabad. His research interests include General Management, Corporate Governance & Strategy, Marketing and Strategic Management. He also served as Visiting Professor at San Diego State University, University of New Orleans, USA, Mahasarakham University, Thailand and Poznan University of Economics and Business, Poland and delivered many lectures in leading Universities in India and abroad.

Prof. Venkata Ramana received many Honours and prestigious Awards –Vemuri Seshayya Sastry Gold Medal for Best Thesis in Economics, Commerce and Management in 1986, AICTE Career Award for Young Teachers in 1999 and “Best Professor in Marketing” in Singapore by CMO Council, Asia, Singapore in 2012 to name a few. In addition, he is on various committees of UGC/AICTE/University Court/Board of Studies.

Prof. Venkata Ramana had the distinction of being chosen to accompany the Hon’ble President of India on an official visit to China during May 24 - 25’ 2016 and was also invited as “Observer” for the launch of Smart model villages at Rashtrapati Bhavan on July 2’ 2016. He was appointed as “National Expert” by Asian Productivity Organisation, Japan (2017-19) and is the Special invitee, CII – Telangana Executive Council and is also Vice-Chairman, Confederation of Indian Industry, (CII) skills.

He has authored 10 books and published over 40 scholarly articles in National and International journals and is a popular speaker at various National and International Conferences.

In August, 2017 in recognition of his contributions to the field of Higher Education in general and Management Education in particular, he was appointed as Vice-Chairman of TSCHE by Government of Telangana and is spearheading the innovation, incubation & entrepreneurship activities at Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE) in addition to policy and perspective planning activities. Prof. V. Venkata Ramana was invited to speak at the International Conference on “Convergence – Content, Media and Technology” held at Thailand between 21 st – 23 rd November, 2018 and also invited to participate in the World Academic Summit organised by Times Higher Education held at National University of Singapore from 25 th -27 th September, 2019.

In September, 2019, Prof. V. Venkata Ramana was selected by MHRD, Government of India to participate in the Leadership for Academicians Program (LEAP) organised by the University of Pennsylvania (UPENN) Graduate School of Education, Philadelphia, PA, United States. He was also invited by Harvard University, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA and other leading Universities to discuss possibilities of collaboration with the Higher Education system in Telangana State and internationalisation of Higher Education.

In February, 2020 Prof. V. Venkata Ramana was invited by ITES, National University of Singapore & Nanyang Technological University to explore possibilities of academic cooperations between Telangana Universities and Singapore Universities.

In November, 2020, Prof. V. Venkata Ramana has been appointed as a Member of the Committee by Ministry of Defence, Govt. of India.
