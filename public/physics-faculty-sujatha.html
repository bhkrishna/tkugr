Assistant Professor
Education
: Ph.D(NIT Warangal) , M.Sc-Andhra University
Email
: cherukurusujathaphys@gmail.com
Address

Mrs.Cherukuru Sujatha had joined the institute in 2016.
Teaching Experience:
  • Working as Faculty Member in Department of Physics from 2016.
  • Worked as an Associate Professor in Kshatriya Engineering College, chepur, Armoor from 2015-2016.
  • Worked as an Assistant Professor, Vignan University,Vadlamudi,Guntur from 2013-2015.
Interests

Area of research
  • Magnetic materials, synthesis of nanomaterials, nanomagnetism, and nanocomposites.
Awards
